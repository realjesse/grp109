Project Purpose:

The main reason for this website is to make things easier for customers. Instead of calling the restaurant, they can:

  • Check the menu and see what dishes we offer.
  • Learn about our ingredients and the freshness of our sushi.
  • Book a table online without waiting on the phone.
  • Find our location, opening hours, and contact details easily.

With this website, we want to attract more customers, make reservations smoother, and give people a great first impression of our restaurant before they even visit.

Why This Website Is Important:

Nowadays, most people search for restaurants online before deciding where to eat. A well-designed website helps us:

  • Show off our sushi dishes with pictures .
  • Make ordering and reservations easier for customers.
  • Stay competitive with other sushi restaurants in the area.

Seattle is known for its amazing food scene, and we want Seattle Sushi to be a top choice for sushi lovers. This website helps us connect with customers and make their dining experience even better.
