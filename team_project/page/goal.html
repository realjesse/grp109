Goals

An overall Japanese restaurant theme

There are areas we definitely hit the Japanese theme, such as the homepage and menu. We used a variety of images to envoke the feeling of a real restaurant.

Reservation system functionality

The functionality works as intended, albiet the error validation could be made more accessible. We could have added text to indicate that the field was invalid, rather than just highlighting the border as red.

Contact system functionality

The functionality works great, and the style also looks good. It works like a real contact form would, with the appropriate fields.

A Japanese menu

We created our own menu and added it as an image to our menu page. It fits the overall theme and adds a realistic flair to the website. A real restaurant may have multiple menus, we chose to only add one.

A dynamic and flexible website

We used a combination of FlexBox, styling, and JavaScript to create a modern style website. This allows for more dynamism with the website.
